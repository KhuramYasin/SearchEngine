(Scott Miller / CTV News) Goderich, Ont. - Goderich rolled out the red carpet for Old St. Nick Saturday night. Thousands of residents lined Courthouse Square as Goderich held its first in-person Santa Claus Parade since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic. Festivities started at 7 p.m. with the traditional lighting of the lights, followed immediately by the parade featuring fire engines, marching bands and Christmas themed floats. It was a busy night for Santa, who also anchored parades in Owen Sound, Harriston, Teeswater, and Exeter.
