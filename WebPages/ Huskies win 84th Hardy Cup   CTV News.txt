Saskatchewan hosted the game for the first time since 2009. Manitoba took a 16-point lead in the first quarter, a lead that would last into the third quarter when the Huskies pulled ahead by outscoring Manitoba 14-0. Saskatchewan earned a trip to Quebec for the 2021 Uteck Bowl where they will take on the Montreal Carabins next Saturday. A win against Montreal would send the Huskies to the Vanier Cup final for the first time since 2006.
