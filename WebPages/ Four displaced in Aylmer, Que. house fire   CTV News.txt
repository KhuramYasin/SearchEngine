(File Photo) Share: Reddit Share Text: GATINEAU, QUE. -- Gatineau fire officials say four people have been displaced following a house fire Saturday night. According to a press release, a neighbour called firefighters to a home on rue Chartrand in the Aylmer sector at around 8:40 p.m. The fire that started on the ground floor was brought under control quickly but it still did an estimated $155,000 in damage. No one was hurt. The Gatineau fire service is investigating the cause of the blaze.
