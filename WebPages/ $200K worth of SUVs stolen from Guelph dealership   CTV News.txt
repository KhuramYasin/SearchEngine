Police said the general manager of a dealership on Woodlawn Road West reported three Infinity vehicles were missing from the lot. The vehicles are a white 2022 Infiniti QX80, a black 2022 Infiniti QX80 and a blue 2021 Infiniti QX80, all of which are worth around $70,000. The thefts remain under investigation. Anyone with information is asked to contact police or Crime Stoppers.
