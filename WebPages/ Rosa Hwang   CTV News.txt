Hwang is the recipient of multiple Canadian Screen Awards and RTDNA awards. Born in Busan, South Korea, Hwang grew up in Calgary and graduated from the University of Calgary with a degree in English and from Mount Royal University with a degree in Communications, specializing in Journalism.
