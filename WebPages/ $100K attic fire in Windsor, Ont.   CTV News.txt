Windsor fire crews respond to an attic fire on Moy Ave., Nov. 21. (Source: Jim Targachoff) Exeter, Ont. - Damage is estimated at $100,000 after a Sunday afternoon fire in Windsor, Ont. Crews were called to the scene in the 1400 block of Moy Avenue around 2 p.m. According to Windsor fire, the blaze was contained to the attic and is not considered suspicious. No injuries have been reported.
