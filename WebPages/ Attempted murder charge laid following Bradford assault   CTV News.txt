Police were called to Holland street West just before 12:30 p.m. after receiving reports of a physical altercation. A 28-year-old man was located on the scene with significant injuries after being assaulted with an edged weapon. The victim remains in serious but stable condition at a Toronto hospital. The accused has been held in custody for a bail hearing. Anyone with information is asked to contact South Simcoe Police or crime stoppers.
