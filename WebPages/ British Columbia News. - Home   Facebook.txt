September 29 · Covid cases update. As of Monday, Sept. 27, 2021, 87.7% (4,064,859) of eligible people 12 and older in B.C. have received their first dose of COVID-19 vaccine and 80.5% (3,729,606) have received their second dose. In addition, 88.2% (3,815,333) of all eligible adults in B.C. have received their first dose and 81.3% (3,515,026) have received their second dose.... Over a three-day period, B.C. is reporting 2,239 new cases of COVID-19, for a total of 184,780 cases in the province: Sept. 24-25: 876 new cases Sept. 25-26: 657 new cases Sept. 26-27: 706 new cases Note: The numbers of total and new cases are provisional due to a delayed data refresh and will be verified once confirmed.
