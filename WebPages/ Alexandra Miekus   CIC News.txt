Alexandra has worked as a television reporter, researcher, associate editor, translator, and university lecturer. An immigrant to Canada herself, Alexandra has been researching and writing about migration, displacement and cultural diversity for almost 20 years. She has presented on these topics in Canada, the United States and Europe. Alexandra is committed to sharing stories about the reality of immigration, the different paths and life stories that make it possible, and about what enables newcomers to successfully integrate into their host communities. In addition to the subject of immigration, Alexandra has written dozens of articles on the art of storytelling, literature, and film. She and her husband share their home with two extraordinary children and countless mountains of books.
