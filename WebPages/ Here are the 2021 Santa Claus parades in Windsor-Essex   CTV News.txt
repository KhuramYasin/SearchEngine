(Courtesy Windsor Parade Corporation / Facebook) Windsor, Ont. - Plans are in the works for Santa Claus parades across Windsor-Essex. Many parades were altered or cancelled last year due to COVID-19 restrictions. Now that the outdoor capacity rules have loosened, more traditional festivities can resume. Here’s a list of the 2021 holiday parades: Kingsville – Nov. 20, 6 p.m. in downtown Kingsville. Amherstburg – Nov. 27, 6 p.m. Route begins at Sandwich Street South to Richmond Street to Dalhousie Street. Leamington – Nov. 27,5 p.m. - 8 p.m. The drive-thru event will be held in Point Pelee National Park. Windsor – Dec. 4, 6.p.m. on Wyandotte Street from Devonshire Road to Alymer Avenue Essex – Dec. 11, 6:30 p.m. Runs down Talbot Street from Fairview Avenue East to Maidstone Avenue West.
