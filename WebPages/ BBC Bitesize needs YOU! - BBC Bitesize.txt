Part of Learn & revise Add to My Bitesize Add to My Bitesize BBC Bitesize needs YOU! We are looking for 15-19 year-olds to be part of our 2022 Mind Set project. We would love to hear from young people who sat their GCSE or National 5 exams in 2019, or those who are due to sit them in 2022. Our previous Mind Set coaches. The Mind Set was the UKâ€™s first national peer-to-peer coaching network for GCSE and National 5 students. Through a series of short films, a team of students offered practical and wellbeing advice on a variety of topics. We are currently casting our new team of Mind Set coaches for 2022 aged between 15-19. We will have two teams of coaches: Those who sat their GCSEs or National 5 exams in 2019 Those who will take these exams in 2022 We are looking for young people who think they would make a good mentor to other students and feel confident sharing their tips on how to survive exam season. We are looking for students who can offer some sound advice to others and would love the opportunity to appear on TV and Radio. Does that sound like you? If so, we'd love for you to get in touch! To apply, please send an application email to mindset@bbc.co.uk Your application email should include: Your full name and date of birth What exams you are due to sit and what grades you hope to achieve OR the date you sat your exams, subjects and grades achieved What your best piece of advice to pass on to other students taking exams would be In less than 100 words, why you would make a great mentor to other students taking exams If you are under 18, you must get permission from a parent or guardian and also include the following information: Parent/guardian name Parent/guardian email address (please CC this email address into your application email so they can give their consent) Parent/guardian phone number The deadline for applications is Wednesday, 1 December.
