Officers were called to the scene at Wyandotte Street west and Rankin Avenue around 2 a.m. Police tell CTV News, preliminary investigation shows the vehicle was possibly traveling at a high rate of speed and appears to have lost control, hitting a utility pole. Police are still trying to notify next of kin. After being closed for 12 hours, Wyandotte Street has re-opened between Randolph Avenue and Partingon Avenue.
